Main Features

  • OneKlik is an online payment facility that allows the Consumer to directly pay for the products/services they purchase through the website/application of a Merchant that cooperates with BCA.
  • By registering for OneKlik on the Consumer’s account/User ID, the Consumer can make multiple payments by just clicking the payment button once (1x) on the Merchant's website/application.
  • Available in 1 currency, namely, Rupiah (IDR).
  • OneKlik conforms with the National Open API Payment Standard (SNAP) established by Bank Indonesia.
  • The following types of BCA accounts can serve as OneKlik source of funds: Tahapan, Tahapan Gold, Tahapan Xpresi, Tapres, Rupiah Current Account for Individual Customers, and other sources of funds according to the provisions hereafter existing at BCA.
  • OneKlik registration can be done on the Merchant's website and/or application, followed by a registration activation process on a BCA’s channel (such as BCA mobile and myBCA).
  • The Customer can use the ‘Atur OneKlik’ feature on the BCA channel to block, unblock, or delete the account, set a daily limit, and change source account.
  • The daily limit for OneKlik is Rp3,000,000/card/day, which is the maximum combined limit applicable to all OneKlik Merchants.
  • The minimum Transaction Amount per Source of Fund is Rp1 (one rupiah), while the maximum Transaction amount is the daily limit for OneKlik.
  • BCA may request additional authorization in the form of an OTP code if the transaction requires additional authorization to meet OneKlik security policy.

Benefits

  • The Customer can make online payments at various Merchants with a seamless and hassle-free experience, anywhere and anytime with just one click.
  • Flexibility in setting the daily limit for OneKlik to control the Customer’s spending.
  • Flexibility in managing the account. The Customer can block, unblock, and delete the account according to the Customer's needs when using the OneKlik account.
  • Robust security measures to protect the balance information of the registered source of fund.

Risks

  • Misuse of information regarding the source of fund, mobile phone number, BCA ID, OTP code, or Consumer’s Account/User ID as a consequence of disclosure of such information, whether intentional or unintentional, by the Customer to other parties through social engineering, phishing, loss of device/ ATM card, etc.
