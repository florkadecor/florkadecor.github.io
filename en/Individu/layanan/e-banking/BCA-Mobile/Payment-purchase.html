m-Payment

Pay various postpaid bills, from phone bills, credit cards, insurance, and many more, directly via m-Payment feature on BCA mobile.

Payment

Pay Phone bills
Pay phone bills without any hassle, thanks to m-Payment on BCA mobile
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Payment’
  • Choose ‘Handphone’
Pay Insurance Premier
Don’t forget to pay your insurance premier for protection, via BCA mobile
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Payment’
  • Choose ‘Asuransi’

Pay Internet Bills
Keep surfing the internet by paying your internet bills via BCA mobile
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Payment’
  • Choose ‘Internet’
Pay Credit Card / BCA Paylater Bills
After shopping, you can pay BCA and other bank credit card bills and BCA Paylater on BCA mobile
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose m-Payment menu
  • Choose Credit Card / BCA Paylater

Pay Telkom/Indihome

  • Open BCA mobile

Pay Water Bills (PAM)
Keep your water flowing at home, by paying PAM bills via BCA mobile
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Payment’
  • Choose ‘Air’
  • Choose ‘PAM’ on the ‘Menu Perusahaan’ column
Pay Now
Pay Electricity Bills (PLN)
Forget to pay for your electricity? Pay immediately via BCA mobile
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Payment’

  • Choose ‘PLN on the ‘Menu Perusahaan’ column
Pay Now
Pay BPJS
Pay BPJS without hassle via BCA mobile
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Payment’
  • Choose one of two choices in ‘Jenis BPJS’ (‘BPJS Kesehatan’ or ‘BPJS Individu’)
Pay Loans
Pay credit loans on smartphone via BCA mobile
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Payment’
  • Choose ‘Pinjaman’
  • Choose a company from the list of ‘Nama Perusahaan’ and input your customer ID number
Pay Cable TV Bills
Keep your Cable TV alive by paying via BCA mobile
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Payment’
  • Choose ‘Menu Lainnya’
  • Choose the Cable TV Company Name on ‘Nama Perusahaan’
Pax Taxes
Pay for land and building taxes (PBB) from anywhere :
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Payment’
  • Choose ‘Pajak’
  • Input Tax Object Number and Tax Year
Other Payment
Pay for other bills including myLPG from BCA mobile is simpler:
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Payment’
  • Choose ‘Lainnya’
  • Choose a company from the list of ‘Nama Perusahaan’
    Company Bills that are available on BCA mobile: Amway, Binus, DPLKINDO, FASPAY, FIRSTMED, MVISION, myLPG, TRANSVSN

m-Commerce

Buy various prepaid vouchers, from phone vouchers, electricity token, to game vouchers, directly via m-Commerce feature on BCA mobile.

Purchase Pre-paid and Google Play Vouchers
Buy pre-paid cellphone credits, data package, or Google Play vouchers via BCA mobile:
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Commerce’
  • Choose ‘Voucher Isi Ulang’
  • Choose ‘Jenis Voucher’
Purchase Electricity Token
Buy pre-paid electricity token is simpler via BCA mobile
  • Open BCA mobile
  • Choose ‘m-Commerce’
  • Choose ‘PLN Prabayar’ or 'PLN Manual Advice'
  • Enter your Meter Number or Customer ID (and transaction code for PLN Manual Advice)
