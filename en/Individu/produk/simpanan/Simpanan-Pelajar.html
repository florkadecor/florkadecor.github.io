SimPel

Tabungan SimPel BCA is a savings product issued nationally by banks in Indonesia for students, which serves as preparatory for the future as well as an early introduction to the banking world.

Important Information

Terms and Procedures:

  • Available for an individual customer.
  • Only for Indonesian citizen.
  • Fill and sign account opening form.
  • Account opening is not subject to stamp duty fee.
  • Bring all necessary documents.

Features, Benefits, and Risks of Simpanan Pelajar

Key Features of Simpanan Pelajar:

  • Available in Rupiah.
  • Simpanan Pelajar does not earn interest.
  • Minimum subsequent deposit of IDR1,000
  • Minimum reserved balance of IDR5,000
  • Maximum balance of IDR20,000000
  • The guaranteed interest rates adhere to the prevailing interest rates on Deposit Insurance Corporations (LPS) webpage.
  • Daily transaction limit based on card:

Benefits:

  • Provided with a Simpanan Pelajar card.
  • Guaranteed security, PIN for ATM BCA allows for a safe and comfortable transaction.

Risks:

  • LPS does not guarantee your savings, if:
    1. The amount of all savings (including interest) at one bank exceeds (equal to) Rp2 billion for both single and joint accounts.
    2. The interest rate applicable exceeds the LPS-guaranteed interest rate.
  • Interest rates fluctuation may occur following market developments.
  • Simpanan Pelajar Account will be dormant if account is inactive/no transactions except, taxes and/or administrative fees for 12 (twelve) consecutive months. If Simpanan Pelajar account is dormant and the account balance reaches ≤ Rp 5,000.00 (five thousand rupiah) then the account can be closed automatically by the system with an account closing fee of the remaining balance.

Additional Information

  • It is not available for joint account.
  • Customers are only allowed to open 1 account.
  • Simpanan Pelajar does not have e-channel facilities.

  1. Simpanan Pelajar means a savings account intended for students and issued nationwide by Indonesian banks, with easy and simple requirements as well as attractive features, for the purposes of financial education and inclusion so as to promote the saving culture at an early age.
  2. Simpanan Pelajar Depositor means a student of PAUD, TK, SD, SMP, SMA, Madrasah (MI, MTs, MA) or equivalent who is under 17 years old and has not obtained a Resident ID Card (KTP) (hereinafter referred to as the “Depositor”).
  3. The Depositor is only allowed to open 1 (one) Simpanan Pelajar account at BCA. The Simpanan Pelajar account can only be opened in the form of a single account (it cannot be opened in the form of a joint account with the status of “And” or “Or”).
  4. Upon opening the Simpanan Pelajar account, BCA will issue Simpanan Pelajar Card as a means to make transactions.   6. The Simpanan Pelajar account will be accorded a dormant status if the Simpanan Pelajar account is inactive, i.e., there is no transaction except taxes and/or administrative fees for 12 (twelve) consecutive months. If the Simpanan Pelajar account is accorded a dormant status and the account balance is less than or equal to Rp5.000,00 (five thousand rupiah), the account may be automatically closed by the system, and an account closing fee in the amount of the remaining balance will be charged.

Such fees will be debited directly by BCA from the Depositor’s Simpanan Pelajar.

B. DEPOSITS AND WITHDRAWALS

  1. The initial deposit must be at least Rp 5,000 (five thousand Rupiah) and the subsequent deposits must be at least Rp 1,000 (one thousand rupiah) or any other amount as notified by BCA from time to time in any form and by any means in accordance with the prevailing law.
  2. Deposits can be made at any time through the teller counter during BCA business hours or through BCA’s car when BCA branch office representatives are visiting the school, or through BCA cash deposit machines and/or other facilities as may be determined by BCA.
  3. Cash withdrawals or funds transfers can be made at any time at a BCA branch office during BCA business hours or through BCA ATMs and/or other facilities as may be determined by BCA.
  4. Cash withdrawal and/or funds transfer transactions can only be conducted up to a maximum of 4 (four) times within the same month for all facilities used by the Depositor/the Depositor’s parent or guardian. BCA has the right to at any time change the provisions regarding the cash withdrawal transaction, and the change will be notified by BCA in any form and by any means in accordance with the prevailing law.
  5. For the cash withdrawals or funds transfers at the counter will be processed through a PIN pad machine using Simpanan Pelajar Card. BCA has the right to ask for original identity card from the Depositor for cash withdrawals through the counter in a certain amount.
  6. Cash withdrawals or funds transfers by the Depositor’s attorney-in-fact can be made at a BCA branch office, and the Depositor’s attorney-in-fact must present a duly fiscal-stamped power of attorney from the Depositor as well as the Depositor’s identity card and the attorney-in-fact’s original identity card.

  3. Any complaints relating to the Simpanan Pelajar account must be lodged by the Depositor with BCA within no later than 3 (three) months of the transaction date.
