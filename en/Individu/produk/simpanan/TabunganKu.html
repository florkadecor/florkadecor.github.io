TabunganKu

TabunganKu BCA is a savings product for individuals that offers simple and easy requirements. Run jointly by banks in Indonesia, the Tabunganku aims to grow culture of saving and improve the community welfare. The Tabunganku account is not subject to monthly administration fee.

Features, Benefits, and Risks of TabunganKu

Key Features of Tabunganku:

  • Available in Rupiah.
  • Minimum subsequent deposit of IDR10,000
  • Minimum reserved balance of IDR20,000
  • Competitive interest rates. For details on interest rates, visit www.bca.co.id.
  • The guaranteed interest rates adhere to the prevailing interest rates on Deposit Insurance Corporations (LPS) webpage.

Benefits:

  • Provided with a passbook and/or a Tabunganku card.
  • Guaranteed security, PIN for ATM BCA allows for a safe and comfortable transaction.

Risks:

  • LPS does not guarantee your savings, if:
    1. The amount of all savings (including interest) at one bank exceeds (equal to) Rp2 billion for both single and joint accounts.
    2. The interest rate applicable exceeds the LPS-guaranteed interest rate.
  • Interest rates fluctuation may occur following market developments.
  • If the remaining balance in the customer account is zero and there are no transactions for 12 consecutive months, the account will be automatically closed.
