BCA Installment

Convert your transactions into BCA installment with a very competitive interest and installment period of up to 36 months, including overseas transactions.

How to convert your purchases into BCA installment:

  • Via myBCA on the Credit Card Information menu
  • At all merchants with Cicilan BCA sign
  • Type cicilan, send SMS to 89888
  • Via BCA mobile (m-Info menu then Access Transaksi dan Tagihan)
    • Make sure the financial transactions in BCA mobile is activated at BCA Branch Office 
    •  Make sure Kartu Kredit BCA is connected with BCA mobile at “m-Admin” menu)
  • Contact Halo BCA 1500888

Autopay BCA

Combining all your bills into one single payment via BCA credit cards. The name listed must be under your own name.

How to activate Autopay BCA :

  1. Type Autopay and send the SMS to 89888
  2. Register through halo BCA 1500888
  3. Fill out the registration form by attaching a copy of ID Card and BCA credit card at BCA branch offices or directly at the Merchant counter. The registration form is also available on the Online Registration web https://webform.bca.co.id/kartu-kredit/daftar-autopay

Contactless

Contactless feature allows for a faster and easier BCA credit card transaction at merchants using EDC. Simply tap your BCA Credit Card on a contactless enabled EDC machine, and your transaction is settled in an instant.

How to Activate Contactless Feature on BCA Mastercard World :

  1. Make sure that your BCA Credit Card is active and has a contactless logo.
  2. By making 1 (one) transaction on EDC machine/ATM using PIN, after that Contactless Feature is active and ready to use.
  3. The Contactless feature on the BCA Mastercard World is active and ready to use.
