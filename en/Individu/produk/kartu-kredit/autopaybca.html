Autopay BCA

A solution for recurring bill payment without lifting a finger. You don’t have to go through the hassle to remember different due dates every month anymore, such as bills for electricity, phone, internet, insurance, or TV subscription.

General Provisions

  • Minimum credit limit of Rp3,000,000
  • The application for registration of Autopay BCA must be carried out by the main cardholder. Specifically for BCA Visa Corporate card, the registration application must be carried out by the PIC of the company
  • The effective Autopay cut-off is maximum 2 months after receiving the Autopay welcome letter
  • If the cut-off dates above fall on holiday (Saturday, Sunday, or national holidays), the cut-off date advances 1 day to the previous business day
  • Cardholders hereby authorize BCA to automatically debit enrolled credit card account, and make the payables to service providers
  • The name listed is not a company name, it must be under your own name
  • BCA, at its sole discretion, can reject any application for Autopay facility
  • In the event of any failure in making the auto-debit, BCA has the right to debit any other active and enrolled BCA credit cards
  • The amount of Autopay Bill is determined by the service provider. If there is a discrepancy between the Autopay Bill and the bill payable, Cardholder shall contact the service provider.
  • Valid for all types of BCA Credit Cards including supplementary cards, except for BCA Visa Singapore Airlines and Corporate Business Account (CBA) cards
  • BCA is not responsible for double bill payments caused by Cardholder errors
  • If the subscription is no longer active, Cardholder is required to notify PT. Bank Central Asia Tbk. via Halo BCA 1500888. If such notification fails to be made, Autopay BCA will remain active and BCA is not responsible for any and all transactions that occur
  • Administration fee for automatic payment see Fees and Limits
