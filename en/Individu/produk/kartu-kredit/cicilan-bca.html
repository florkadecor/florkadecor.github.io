About

Shop worry-free with Cicilan BCA! Convert your credit card transaction into installment with interest start from 0% and tenure up to 36 months on myBCA and BCA mobile.

BCA is partnering with various merchants to provide convenience and shopping relief with the Cicilan BCA program. Convert your credit card transactions directly at BCA EDC with interest rates starting at 0% and tenures up to 12 months.

Convert Your Purchases into Cicilan BCA

  • Via myBCA

  1. Make sure the myBCA application has been updated to version 1.20

  2. Choose Akun Saya, select Informasi Kartu Kredit

  3. Choose the transaction you want to convert to installment, select Ubah ke Cicilan

  4. Choose Jangka Waktu Cicilan, select Lanjut

  5. Check the change details, check the statement and select Lanjut

  6. Submission of transaction change to installment successfully submitted

  • Via BCA mobile

  1. Select m-BCA

  2. Select menu m-Info, Info Kartu Kredit, then select Transaksi dan Tagihan

  3. Choose Ubah ke Cicilan on the transaction you want to convert to installments

  4. Choose Jangka Waktu Cicilan

  5. Check the change details, check the statement and select Ubah ke Cicilan

  6. Submission of transaction change to installment successfully submitted

  7. Make sure the financial transactions in BCA mobile is activated at BCA Branch Office or Halo BCA

  8. Make sure your BCA Credit Card is connected to BCA mobile

  • At merchants with BCA Installment information

  • Type Cicilan send SMS to 89888

  • Contact Halo BCA 1500888
