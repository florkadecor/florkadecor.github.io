Reward BCA

Reward BCA is a BCA’s loyalty point program, where BCA Credit Cardholders can earn Reward BCA for every transaction made using BCA Credit Card in Indonesia and overseas. Accumulated Reward BCA can be used as a direct deduction when you shop, or exchange to airline miles or eCommerce/Lifestyle partners, and many more!

Earn Reward BCA for every transactions made with BCA Credit Card:

As an illustration, when a Cardholder spent IDR500,000 with BCA Card Platinum Credit Card, Cardholder will earn IDR1,500 Reward BCA (0.3% x IDR500,000).

Important Notice

  • In the event that any transaction is voided/cancelled/disputed, BCA reserves the right to cancel and void any Reward BCA awarded to Cardholder.
  • Cash advance and credit card fees will not be eligible for Reward BCA earning.
  • Effective 12 August 2024, payments under the categories of donations (including social services), government services (including tax payments, customs, immigration fees, and BPJS), as well as electricity, water & gas payments, will no longer earn Reward BCA, KrisFlyer miles, Blibli Tiket Point, or Blipay
  • All transactions made with BCA Mastercard and Visa Credit Cards on or after August 12, 2024, will be subject to the terms mentioned above.
  • Transactions under the Merchant Category Code (MCC) as per the provisions of Mastercard and Visa will not earn rewards

Check Reward BCA Balance

1. Via myBCA

  • Choose 'Akun Saya'
  • Reward BCA, Reward AMEX, and Reward UnionPay will appear in the 'Reward' menu
  • Choose 'Reward' for detailed information

2. Via BCA mobile

  • Choose menum-Info
  • Choose 'Info Reward BCA', and enter your m-BCA PIN
  • Information on Reward BCA will appear

3. Via BCA Website

  • Visit bca.id/ceksaldoreward
  • Enter your BCA Credit Card number and date of birth
  • Information on Reward BCA will appear

4. Via Halo BCA 1500888 or call feature on haloBCA mobile application

5. Via EDC BCA
