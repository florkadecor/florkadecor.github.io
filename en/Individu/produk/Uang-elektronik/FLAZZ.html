Flazz

A multifunctional transaction card with RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) chip technology that simplifies and speeds up the non-cash payment process.

Benefits

Secure
Transaction privacy is maintained with payments made electronically
Detail
Recorded transaction details make it easier for merchants to carry out cash handling and transaction reporting
Various Transaction
Use it to pay for national toll roads, parking, public transportation (Transjakarta & Transjogja, MRT Jakarta, LRT Jakarta, LRT Jabodebek, LRT Sumsel, Commuter Line Jabodetabek, Commuter Line Jogja Solo, and others), SPBU, shopping at mini markets and transactions in more from 57 thousand other merchant outlets.
Thousands of merchant
Flazz cards can be used to make transactions at more than 57 thousand merchant outlets

Important Information

  • Cardholder is an individual who has a Flazz Card.
  • Flazz Card is a card-shaped payment instrument that can be used for payment transactions by debiting funds stored on a Flazz Card
  • Transactions are Payment Transactions, Top Up Transactions, and other transactions conducted by Cardholders using a Flazz Card
  • When conducting payment transaction, Flazz Card must be placed on the reader
  • Please keep Flazz Card in a safe location to prevent damage due to breakage, scratch, or heat

General Terms and Conditions

  1. The Flazz Card is transferrable to any party.
  2. The Flazz Card cannot be used for purposes other than to conduct a Transaction.
  3. The Flazz Card can be used without a Personal Identification Number (PIN) or a signature. Therefore, the Flazz Card can be used by any person other than the Cardholder, without the need for BCA to prove the user’s authority.
  4. If the Flazz Card is stolen or lost, BCA cannot block the funds on the Flazz Card. All consequences arising from the use of the stolen or lost Flazz Card shall be the full responsibility of the Cardholder.
  5. The Cardholder can only use the Flazz Card to make Payment Transactions as long as the funds on the Flazz Card are sufficient.
  6. All information and calculations from BCA in connection with the Payment Transactions, other transactions and/or the Flazz Card balance after the use of the Flazz Card constitute binding and conclusive evidence, unless proven otherwise.
  7. The use of the Flazz Card is subject to the terms and provisions applicable at BCA as well as the terms and conditions governing all services or facilities and transactions covered by the Flazz Card. BCA reserves the right to amend the terms, conditions, and provisions, and such amendment will be notified by BCA in any form and by any means in accordance with the applicable law.
  8. In the event of a force majeure event, including but not limited to strikes, fires, mass riots, sabotage, natural disasters such as earthquakes and floods or other occurrences beyond the control of the parties, no party shall be liable for any losses that arise as a result of the occurrence of such force majeure event.
  9. The Flazz Card does not constitute a deposit as referred to in the banking law, and therefore the funds on the Flazz Card are not guaranteed by the Indonesia Deposit Insurance Corporation (LPS).
  10. The validity period of the Flazz Card is 10 (ten) years from the last Top Up Transaction. However, the validity period of the Flazz Card with the new logo is 10 years from the production date as written on the back of the card
