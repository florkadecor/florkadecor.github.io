TERMS AND CONDITIONS FOR KLIKBCA BISNIS PT BANK CENTRAL ASIA Tbk (BCA)

A. Registration for KlikBCA Bisnis

  1. Any Customer that owns a business and has deposit account(s) with BCA may use the KlikBCA Bisnis facility.
  2. To enjoy the KlikBCA Bisnis facility, a Customer must have a user ID for KlikBCA Bisnis (Corporate ID and User ID), KeyBCA, and Personal Identification Number (PIN) to activate the KeyBCA and Virtual Private Network (VPN) Application, all of which are obtainable by the Customer upon registration for KlikBCA Bisnis.

B. KlikBCA Bisnis Facility/Service

  1. The KlikBCA Bisnis facility/service shall be provided according to the type of service package elected by the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer as set forth in the KlikBCA Bisnis Application Form.
  2. BCA may at anytime, on certain considerations, change the operation manual for KlikBCA Bisnis and will notify the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer of such change in any form and by any means in accordance with the laws in force.
  3. KlikBCA Bisnis Customer is prohibited from using KlikBCA Bisnis facility/service to perform actions that violate or conflict with the norms of decency, religion, morals, public order, law, or applicable laws and regulations.
  4. Without prior written approval from BCA, KlikBCA Bisnis Customer is prohibited from using KlikBCA Bisnis facility/service for the purpose of offering commercial services to other parties, among others services as fund transfer providers.

C. Other User ID Holders

  1. The KlikBCA Bisnis Customer may provide its nominee(s) with access to KlikBCA Bisnis on the full responsibility of the relevant KlikBCA Bisnis Customer.
  2. The KlikBCA Bisnis Customer which elects 'Single Authorization' shall grant access to KlikBCA Bisnis to the owner/holder of the User ID as set out in the KlikBCA Bisnis Application Form.
  3. The KlikBCA Bisnis Customer which elects 'Multi-Authorization' shall grant access to KlikBCA Bisnis to Sysadmin1 and Sysadmin2 as set out in the KlikBCA Bisnis Application Form to request the registration of other User IDs in the application provided to the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer.
  4. The level and limit on the transaction amount for each User ID owner/holder shall be determined by the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer or any person appointed by the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer.

D. Accounts, Credit Cards, and Merchant IDs

  1. Any account for transaction information (an account allowing the Customer to access information on the balance and/or history thereof through KlikBCA Bisnis) and any account for debit (a debitable account) must first be registered with BCA. All types of accounts opened at BCA can be registered for KlikBCA Bisnis.
  2. Any account with the status of 'guardian' or 'trustee' cannot be registered.
  3. Credit cards that can be registered on KlikBCA Bisnis by the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer are only those issued by BCA.
  4. The KlikBCA Bisnis Customer can register its merchant ID to KlikBCA Bisnis. Registration of the merchant ID allows the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer to download a report in the form as determined by BCA on transactions conducted on the EDC machine under the relevant merchant ID.
  5. Registration of any account including joint account, with ‘AND’ or ‘OR’ status, credit card, and merchant ID to KlikBCA Bisnis in a name different from the name of the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer must be accompanied by a power of attorney from the relevant account holder, credit cardholder, or merchant ID holder.
  6. The power of attorney shall contain the powers granted to the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer to access and conduct transactions using the account, credit card, and merchant ID specified in the power of attorney.
  7. The KlikBCA Bisnis Customer must be the same entity as the primary account holder registered on KlikBCA Bisnis by the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer. Joint account with ‘AND’ status must not be designated as the primary account
  8. BCA reserves the right to delete any account registered on KlikBCA Bisnis of the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer if for some reason the account is closed.
  9. If the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer closes its primary account or if, for some reason, its primary account is closed, the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer must designate a new primary account to replace the closed primary account. If the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer fails to designate a new primary account, the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer hereby agrees that BCA may designate any other account owned by the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer as the primary account.

E. Limit

  1. The daily limit on the transaction amount for each type of transaction is determined by the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer as set forth in the KlikBCA Bisnis Application Form subject to the terms and conditions applied by BCA. Notwithstanding the above provisions, the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer who obtains credit facility which has been registered on KlikBCA Bisnis, can pay the debt incurred from the credit facility through KlikBCA Bisnis in accordance with the daily limit determined by BCA which can be accessed on Transaction Limit Usage menu on KlikBCA Bisnis.
  2. In addition to the limit determined by the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer, there are certain transactions whose limit shall be determined according to the policy or procedure stipulated by Bank Indonesia, the Financial Services Authority (OJK), or any other competent authorities, or BCA. On certain considerations, BCA may change the daily limit on the transaction amountand/or transaction limit and shall notify the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer of the same in any form and by any means according to the prevailing law.

To access KlikBCA Bisnis, the KlikBCA Bisnis Customer must:
