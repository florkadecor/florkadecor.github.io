Important Information

Card Security Measures

  • Ensure that your BCA Visa Corporate Card is always under your control by checking its existence regularly. When the card is expired, immediately cut it in half through the chip and magnetic stripe
  • Make sure you get your BCA Visa Corporate Card back right after each transaction. Check the name on the card and never lend your BCA Visa Corporate Card to others because you are fully responsible for the use of your BCA Visa Corporate Card
  • Immediately sign your BCA Visa Corporate Card. Be careful with your card information and don’t give your BCA Visa Corporate Card information to strangers, especially over the phone
  • Don’t reveal your PIN (Personal Identification Number) to anyone or record or write the PIN in documents or media that are easily known or seen by others.
  • If your BCA Visa Corporate Card cannot be used, take the card to the nearest BCA branch office, cut it up through the chip and magnetic stripe. BCA never authorizes anyone to take your BCA Visa Corporate Card from you

If there is a transaction that is not your transaction, immediately submit a question or objection in writing to layanan_korporasi@bca.co.id by the Corporate PIC not exceeding 30 (thirty) calendar days from the billing date. To block the Visa Corporate Credit Card, immediately report to Halo BCA 1500888.

Keep your BCA Visa Corporate Card number and Halo BCA phone number handy for the convenience of reporting in case your BCA Visa Corporate Card is lost or stolen

If your BCA Visa Corporate Card is lost or stolen, immediately report the loss/theft to Halo BCA 1500888. You are fully responsible for all transactions that occur before BCA receives the loss/theft report

Contactless

Contactless feature allows for a faster and easier BCA credit card transaction at merchants using EDC. Simply tap your BCA Credit Card on a contactless enabled EDC machine, and your transaction is settled in an instant.

How to Activate Contactless Feature on BCA Visa Corporate:

  1. Make sure that your BCA Credit Card is active and has a contactless logo.
  2. By making 1 (one) transaction on EDC machine/ATM using PIN, after that Contactless Feature is active and ready to use.
  3. The Contactless feature on the BCA Visa Corporate is active and ready to use.
